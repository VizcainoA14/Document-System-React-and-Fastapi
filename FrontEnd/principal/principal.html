About us?

We are a company dedicated to the provision of document management services where you as users can make purchases or book rentals.

Our users will be able to interact with the system either making a purchase or renting a document. In the same way, it can request authentication to the system. And we, as management support, can create, delete and update the documents so that we can also classify them according to their characteristics, since they can be magazines, monographs, essays, audiobooks, among others.
